Biography

Hi, I'm Brian. I'm a roboticist who wants to make robots do useful work in the real world. I'm especially interested in leveraging optimization, control theory, and machine learning to allow robots to operate in challenging enironments. My current research focuses on designing efficient, robust perception and control algorithms for dynamic bipedal walking over rough terrain, and successfully deploying these algorithms on hardware.
